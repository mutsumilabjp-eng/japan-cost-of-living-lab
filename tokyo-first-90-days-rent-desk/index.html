How I built the numbers

The current ward reference uses A-Realty's May 2026 analysis of 26,874 active 1K listings, with at least 80 listings per ward. Two LIFULL HOME'S listing-history pages provide specific building spot checks. The Statistics Bureau Housing and Land Survey is used only as official context, not as a live asking-rent feed. MLIT guidance and a market-practice guide inform the lease-process and fee checklist.

The workbook adds a transparent +/-15% shortlist band, a ¥10,000 management-fee buffer, and four-to-six-month housing cash stress. These are planning assumptions, not property guarantees. Replace them with live listings and the written agent quote before applying.
