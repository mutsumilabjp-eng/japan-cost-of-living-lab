About Japan Cost of Living Lab

I'm Mutsumi, a local editor in Japan. I run Japan Cost of Living Lab for people who need to make a costly moving decision from information scattered across Japanese sources, listing sites, and broad averages.

I am not an expat and I do not pretend to have lived every moving scenario I write about. My job is to read the Japanese material, check when the number was published, show the assumptions, and say where the evidence stops.

What I investigate

  • Rent and the cash due before move-in
  • Monthly budgets and paycheck timing
  • Salary, utilities, groceries, phone, and transport
  • The first 90 days after arrival
  • Reusable sheets for comparing a real decision

I do not try to prove that Japan is cheap or expensive. I separate recurring costs from setup cash, then test one decision at a time: Can this salary carry this rent? How much cash is needed before keys? What changes when two written quotes are compared?

The free guides complete one useful example. Paid tools add broader comparisons, editable inputs, and updates for readers who have reached a real deadline.

Every guide has a last-checked date. I link to official or primary material where it exists, separate survey context from live listing references, and correct material errors when I find them.
