Privacy

Last updated: July 24, 2026.

Japan Cost of Living Lab collects an email address when you choose to join Japan Cost Update. The calculator may also send the diagnosis label and rounded planning amounts shown on screen so the requested result can be included in the email.

What is collected

  • Your email address
  • The page or tool where you subscribed
  • If you subscribe from the calculator: Safe, Tight, or High Risk; the estimated cash gap; and the estimated cash needed before the first full paycheck

Do not enter confidential employer, bank, immigration, tax, or identity information into the calculator or email form.

Why it is used

The information is used to send the checklist, the requested calculator result, Japan cost updates, and relevant planning guidance. It is not sold.

Email provider

Email subscriptions and delivery are handled by Kit. Kit processes subscriber data under its own privacy and security terms.

Your choices

Every marketing email includes an unsubscribe link. You may unsubscribe at any time. To request access, correction, or deletion of subscriber information, use the reply address shown in a Japan Cost Update email.

Planning-tool limits

Calculator inputs and outputs are estimates, not financial, tax, legal, housing, or immigration advice. Confirm apartment fees and payroll timing with the relevant provider or employer.
